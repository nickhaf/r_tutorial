Data sets

We will use two data sets during this workshop. One for the theory, and one for you to work on in the exercises.

Theory: Olympic athletes

1

For the theory part of the workshop, we will mainly work with the athletes data set, downloaded from here. It contains the Olympic athletes from 1896 to 2016, along with some basic stats, their sport and country, and the medals they won. Our goal for the theory part of this workshop is to plot the number of medals by country in each sport, and learn R along the way!

Exercises: Fictional characters

2

Over the course of this workshop, you can work on exercises to train the theoretical knowledge acquired in the chapters. Most of these exercises will use the characters data set, downloaded from here, which contains psychometric ratings for different fictional characters, rated by a large number of people on a personality scale developed by the author of the questionnaire.

You will load the data, prepare it for analyses and also plot it in the end.
